Modular Drone Platform

Live video, remote control, route uploads, and user management — in one platform.

Airlane helps you operate a reliable drone fleet at scale. Stream low-latency video over the internet, control vehicles in real time, upload mission routes, manage users & roles, and integrate with your tools.

Features

Everything you need to run a professional drone operation.

Video

Live streaming over the internet

Low-latency video via WebRTC with fallback. Adaptive bitrate, server-side recording, and playback links.

Control

Remote control

Real-time commands for motion, camera gimbal, and payload. Joystick & keyboard support with rate limiting.

Routes

Route uploads & mission planning

Create, simulate, and upload routes. Pause, resume, geofencing & no-fly zones, and mission logs.

Users

User & role management

Granular permissions: viewer, pilot, admin. SSO-ready. Audit trails for all actions.

Fleet

Fleet management

Register vehicles, monitor status, battery health, GPS & telemetry. Alerts for anomalies.

Integrations

APIs & webhooks

REST, WebSocket, and webhook callbacks. Export media and telemetry to your storage or analytics stack.

How it works

Connect your drone, stream video, plan routes, and go.

1

Connect & register

Install the agent on your Linux-based drone or companion computer. Register it with a secure token.

2

Stream & control

Open a live session to stream video and issue commands from anywhere with internet access.

3

Plan & execute missions

Upload routes, set waypoints, and execute missions with live telemetry and automatic logging.
